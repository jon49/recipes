tags:

categories:
    - recipes
---

Coat chicken breasts with

```
1/4 cup oil
3 T brown sugar
3 t. salt
1 1/2 t cinnamon
1/2 t thyme
1/2 t allspice
1/4 t cayenne
```

Broil on pan lined with foil
6 min. on each side
