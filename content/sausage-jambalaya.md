---
title: Sausage Jambalaya
description: Recipe for Sausage Jambalaya.
tags:
    - dinner
categories:
    - recipes
---

Brown 2 Lb. pork sausage. Add 1 onion, cook 5 mins.

Add

```
1 diced bell pepper
2 celery ribs
Two cloves garlic
Cook 5 minutes.
```

Stir in

```
Riced cauliflower
Bay leaf
1 t. thyme
Paprika
Oregano
```

Cook 3 min

Add

```
8 cups broth
2 cans tomatoes
```

Simmer 20 min. Top with 6 scallions
