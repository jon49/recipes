Sautee

```
1 medium onion, diced
6 stalks celery, diced
6 medium carrots, diced
```

Add, then simmer 1 - 1 1/2 hours

```
4 bay leaves
2 T. fresh thyme (or 2 t. dry)
Salt/pepper
4 cups split peas
12 cups broth
Ham bone
```

When peas are soft, remove ham meat from bone and return to pot.

Crock pot: Put all ingredients to crock pot. Cook 5-6 hours on high.
