---

Sautee

```
1 chopped onion
6 T. celery tops
```

Add and cook

```
2 lb chopped chicken
```

Make layers on top

```
2 med. chopped Potatos
8 med. chopped carrots
1/2 head chopped cabbage
5 c. chopped green beans
2 chopped tomatos
```

Add 2 c. water and S and P

Simmer 30 min.

Sprinkle with 4 T. parsley
