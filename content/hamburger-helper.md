---

Cook

```
2 lbs ground beef
```

Add

```
2 cups pasta
Diced cauliflower
2 1/2 cups milk
1 1/2 cups water
1 T. cornstarch
1 T. chili powder
1 t. sugar
1 t. salt
3/4 t. paprika
1/4 t. cayenne
Pinch of crushed red pepper
```

Cook 10-12 mins, until pasta done

Add

```
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
```
