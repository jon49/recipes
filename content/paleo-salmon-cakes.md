Combine

```
1 1/2 pounds salmon cooked, chopped
1/2 c. sweet potato  puree
4 eggs
1/4 cup coconut flour
1/2 C. green onions, sliced
4 tsp. mustard
Salt and pepper
```

Form 12 patties and bake 30 min. at 350* on parchment paper lined baking sheet
