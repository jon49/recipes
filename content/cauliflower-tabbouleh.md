Pulse 1 head of cauliflower in food processor. Place in bowl.

Pulse and mix into cauliflower

```
6 green onions
2 large tomatoes
1 cucumber
1 bunch parsley
Handful of mint leaves
```

For dressing, whisk

```
Juice of 2 lemons
1/4 cup oil
1 t. salt, pepper
```

Combine pulsed vegetables, chopped avocado, and dressing.
