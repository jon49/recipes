Chop and cook in coconut oil

```
2 zucchini
1 bunch spinach/kale
```

Add

```
Basil
Parsley
Chives
2 cloves garlic
1/2 c. raw pepitas
```

Whisk and add.

```
10 eggs
```

Optional - add cream
