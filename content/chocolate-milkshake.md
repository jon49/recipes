*2 Servings*

Blend

```
1 1/2 cups ice
1 cup unsweetened almond milk
1 cup heavy cream
1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa
1/4 cup granulated low carb sweetener (or banana - not low carb!)
```

Garnish (optional)

```
Whipped cream
Chopped toasted nuts
Sprinkle unsweetened cocoa powder
```
