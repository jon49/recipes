Mix

```
1 Cup Greek Yogurt (No Added Sugar)
1/4 Cup Sour Cream
2 Tbsp Heavy Whipping Cream (optional to make it more runny)
1 Tbsp Lemon Juice
1 tsp Celtic Salt
1/2 tsp Chives
1/2 tsp Dill Weed
1/2 tsp Parsley
1/2 tsp Onion Powder
1/2 tsp Garlic Powder
1/2 tsp Black Pepper
```
