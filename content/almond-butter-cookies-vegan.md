_Makes 12 to 16 Cookies_

Mix and leave aside for 10 to 15 minutes (3 flaxseed eggs).

```
3 tbsps ground flaxseeds
9 tbsps warm water
```

Mix with "flax eggs" above.

```
1/2 cup (60g) ground almond flour
1 cup (90g) desiccated coconut
1/4 cup (45g) melted coconut oil
2 tbsps almond butter 
1/2 tsp baking soda
2 tbsps stevia
Splash of vanilla
Almond flakes (optional)
1 cup (90g) chopped pecans (or walnuts)
```

Place them on the baking tray lined with baking sheet (no greasing required). Sprinkle almond flakes on top of the cookies.

Bake the cookies in 180 C (350 F) preheated oven for about 20 minutes. Take them out and leave to cool for 15mins.
