---

Brown in oil.

```
Chorizo
```

Add

```
1/2 minced onion
4 cloves garlic
2 cups blackeye peas
Salt/pepper
```

Sprinkle with.

```
Lemon juice
Parsley
```

For Fish:

Season fish with salt and pepper.

Heat pan and cook on one side.

Flip and top with lemon slices. Finish cooking.
