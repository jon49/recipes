*Serves 1*

Mix and leave overnight in air tight container

```
⅓ cup oats
½ cup almond milk
1 teaspoon chia seeds
½ tablespoon maple syrup
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
```

Stir again in the morning

Top with

```
2 tablespoons slivered almonds
½ medium banana, sliced
⅓ cup blueberries
```
