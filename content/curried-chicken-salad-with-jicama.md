Steam until done (12-15 minutes) then cut up.

```
2 lb chicken breast
```

Combine

```
Cut chicken pieces
2 chopped celery stalks
1 cup grated jicama
1 cup diced green bell pepper
```

Add

```
6 T. apple cider vinegar
6 T. mayonnaise
```

Add

```
1/2 tsp. salt
1 t. pepper
1/2 t. stevia powder
1 t. curry powder
```
