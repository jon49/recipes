Chop

```
2 cucumbers
```

Process until coarsely chopped

```
1 onion
2 yellow oinions
4 lb tomatoes
```

Combine and add

```
6 T. olive oil
4 cups broth?
2 T. balsamic vinegar
1 T. dried basil
```

```
Salt and pepper.
```

Refrigerate 3 hours
