---

Mix

```
2 cups of active sourdough starter or discard
2 eggs
¼ cup oil - I use avocado oil
½ tsp salt
1 tsp cinnamon
2 Tbls honey or maple syrup
2 tsp vanilla
```

Mix but don't over mix with

```
1 tsp baking soda
```

Makes yummy waffles or pancakes!
