Coarsely chop

```
Large head cauliflower
6 large carrots
1 large onion
```

Put vegetables into pot with

```
3 t. curry powder
1 t. garlic powder
1 t. ground ginger
1 1/2 t. turmeric
1/4 t. black pepper
4 cups broth
```

Simmer 30 minutes, until vegetables are tender.

## Chicken and Apple Mixture

Cook in a pan.

```
1 lb chunks of chicken breast
```

Add

```
2 lg diced apples
1/4 t. salt
1/4 t. ground ginger
1/4 t. ground cinnamon
1 T. coconut oil
```

Stir, turn off heat

Add to vegetables and then puree. Stir in chicken and apple mixture.

```
2 cups coconut milk
```

Garnish with

```
2 T. parsley
```
