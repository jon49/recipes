Thinly slice

```
4 portabella mushrooms (or 8 shiitake)
```

Bring to a boil, add mushrooms. Simmer 4 min.

```
8 cups broth
```

In a small bowl mix

```
8 T miso
8 T soy sauce
```

Put into soup with

```
2/3 cup died firm tofu
```

Cook 1 min. Do not boil.

Serve with 4 sliced green onions
