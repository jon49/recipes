*Prep time: 5 min. | cooking time: 10 min. | makes 5 pancakes*

Mix

```
1¼ cups all purp flour
1 Tbs Baking Powder
1 Tbs Sugar
½ tsp salt
```

Add to above and Mix

```
1 egg
1/4 cup oil
1¼ cup or 1 cup Water
```
