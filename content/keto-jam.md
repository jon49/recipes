Mix, Set stove heat to med-high

```
1 Cup Water
1 Tbsp Lemon Juice
```

Add when water is hot

```
2/3 cup allulose
12 ounces of black berries (or any other berry)
```

Either mash the blackberries or use an immersion blender.

Simmer for ~10 minutes.

Add (whisking vigorously)

```
1/2 Tbsp xanthan gum
```

_Alternatively_ add

```
2 Tbsp Chia Seeds
```

Let the mixture sit for ~10 minutes to cool a bit.

Pour into a good sized jar with a lid and put in fridge.

Should be ready to use in four to six hours
