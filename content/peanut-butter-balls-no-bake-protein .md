---

Mix

```
½ cup creamy peanut butter
½ cup maple syrup
```

Mix

```
½ cup almond flour
¼ cup flaxseed meal
½ cup coconut flour plus 2 tablespoons
```

Mix all together.

Roll into balls.

Optional, roll in coconut flakes or chia seeds.
