Cook

```
1 and 1/2 lb pork lion
12 oz kielbasa sausage
1/2 cup onion
2 choppd celery ribs
2 chopped small carrots
```

When the meat is cooked, add

```
2 cloves garic
1/2 t. paprika
```

Stir in

```
4 cups broth
2 chopped green apples
Cabbage
2 sm. chopped red potato
1/2 t. 5 spice
```

Simmer 15 min.

```
Stir in 2 t. honey
```
