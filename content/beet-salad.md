Prepare sour cream dressing

```
1/2 cup sour cream or substitute olive oil
1/2 cup mayonnaise
2 t. dill
2 t. mustard
1/4 t. celery seed
Salt
```

Combine

```
Cut and cooked beets
Chopped apple
Sour cream dressing
Scallions
Spinach
```
