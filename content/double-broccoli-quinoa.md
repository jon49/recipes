Steam

```
5 cups broccoli
```

Cook

```
1 1/2 quinoa cups dry
```

## Make pesto

Puree

```
1/2 cups almonds
2 cups steamed broccoli
3 cloves garlic
2 pinches salt
2 T. lemon juice
Drizzle in olive oil
```

## Combine

```
3 cups cooked quinoa
Rest of broccoli
Pesto
```

Adjust flavors. Serve with avocado, chile oil, chopped almonds.
