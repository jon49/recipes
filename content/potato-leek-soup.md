---
title: Potato/Leek Soup
description: Recipe for Potato/Leek Soup .
tags:
    - dinner
categories:
    - recipes
---

Combine in pot

```
3 med. sliced Potatos
3 med. sliced leek
4 1/2 c. chicken broth
11 oz water
```

Simmer 35-45 mins. Blend soup. Add

```
1/2 c. almond milk
2 t. salt 1/4 t. pepper
```

Garnish with chives

---
