---
title: Whole Wheat Vegan Cookies
description: Recipe for Whole Wheat Vegan Cookies.
tags:
  - dessert
categories:
    - recipes
---

Heat oven to 375&deg;

Mix

```
2 1/4 Cup Flour (Whole Wheat)
1 tsp Salt
1 tsp Baking Powder
1 Tbs Dry Milk
```

Mix

```
1/2 Cup Warm Water
1/2 Cup Honey
```

Crush

```
1/2 Cup Beans
```

Mix All & Add:

```
1 Cup Chocolate Chips
1 tsp Vanilla
```

Cook for 11 minutes
