Brown

```
1 1/2 lb chorizo (or 3 9-oz tubes)
1 lb ground beef
```

Add

```
1 large red onion, diced
```

Add, then simmer

```
3 acorn squash, pre-cooked
3 lg bell peppers, diced
1 poblano pepper, diced
28 oz diced tomatoes
2 cups black beans
1-2 T. salt
2 T. taco seasoning
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. cayennne
```

Garnish with

```
Cilantro
Green onions
Yogurt
Cheese
Avocado, tossed in lime juice
```
