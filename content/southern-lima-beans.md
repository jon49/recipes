tags:

categories:

---

Soak overnight or 1 hr quick.

```
2 lb lima beans
```

Put lima beans in crock pot with

```
3 browned ham shanks
1 chopped onion
1 T. garlic
Broth/water to cover
```

After 3 hours on low, add

```
Yellow squash
Salt/pepper
```
