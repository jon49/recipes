title: Cranberry Sauce
description: Recipe for Cranberry Sauce.
tags:
    - sauce
categories:
    - recipes
---

Boil

```
1 cup sugar or 1/3 cup Swerve
1 cup water or substitute some with orange juice
```

Add

```
12 oz (4 cups whole fresh cranberries)
```

Simmer 10 minutes

Add (optional)

```
1 T. lime juice
Pumpkin pie spice
Bluberry
```
