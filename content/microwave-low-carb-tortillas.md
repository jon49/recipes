*4 Servings*

Mix

```
2 eggs
1 Tbsp almond flour
1 Tbsp lupin flour (or ground flax seed)
1 Tbsp Psyllium Husks
3 Tbsp Heavy Whipping Cream
```

Place on non-stick silicone mat. Don't make it too thin (it will tear) or too
thick.

Microwave for around 90-100 seconds
