---

Sautee

```
1 medium onion, chopped
6 garlic cloves, minced
1 large potato, diced
```

Add, then simmer 10 mins

```
2 chopped bell peppers
1 can diced tomatoes
2 T. chili powder
1/2 t. cumin
2 cups water
```

Stir in 6 cups black beans. (Optional: add corn). Heat and add salt.
