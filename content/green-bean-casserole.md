---

Steam and cut in half

```
2 lb green beans
```

Sautee

```
2 chopped shallots
4 cloves garlic
```

Add and then cook 5-7 minutes

```
2 cups mushrooms, chopped
1/4 cup flour, sprinkled on
1 1/2 cups broth
2 cups almond milk
```

Add

```
1/2 cup fried onion
```

Combine all ingredients in a baking pan. Top with 1 cup fried onion. Bake at 400 deg F for 15 minutes.
