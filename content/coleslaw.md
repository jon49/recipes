Serves 4

Shred

```
4 cups cabbage and 2 cups carrot
```

For dressing:

Whisk

```
1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
4 cloves garlic
1 t. salt, pepper
1 t. dry mustard, celery seed
1 cup mayonnaise
```

Combine vegetables and dressing.
